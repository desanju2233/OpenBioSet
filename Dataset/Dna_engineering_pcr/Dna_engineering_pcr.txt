Protocol details: Perform a DNA engineering PCR with an experimental reaction and a negative control. For the experimental reaction, dispense 27 units of water, 1 unit of forward primer, 1 unit of reverse primer, 1 unit of template DNA, and 20 units of master mix, mixing them sequentially. For the negative control, dispense 28 units of water, 1 unit of forward primer, 1 unit of reverse primer, and 20 units of master mix, mixing them sequentially. Perform an initial denaturation by heating both reactions at 94C for 4 minutes. Repeat the following thermal cycle 35 times for both reactions: heat at 94C for 1 minute, anneal at 55C for 1 minute, and extend at 72C for 1 minute. Follow with a final extension at 72C for 10 minutes for both. Finally, hold both reactions at 4C for 24 hours.
