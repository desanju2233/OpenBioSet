Protocol details: Perform a direct competitive ELISA for morphine detection in urine. Dispense 20 units of the urine sample into the enzyme-coated well and mix. Dispense 100 units of morphine conjugate into the well, mix for 60 seconds, and incubate at 23C for 60 minutes. After incubation, dispose of the reaction liquids. Perform a wash cycle by repeating the following steps 6 times: dispense 350 units of distilled water into the well, mix for 45 seconds, and dispose of the wash water. To develop the color, dispense 100 units of TMB substrate into the well, mix, and incubate at 23C for 30 minutes. Halt the reaction by dispensing 100 units of stop reagent and mixing for 60 seconds. Finally, detect fluorescence on the completed assay for 30 minutes before final disposal.
